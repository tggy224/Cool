A Remote Control Car Activity

Yesterday I got to solder a remote control car ! Papa helped me by putting the wood pieces together, and I twisted the wires together. Papa said we needed to solder it, so we went to go get the soldering iron and the rosin wire . BUT, the rosin wire had disappeared! It was not there! Mama had to ask Cindy A-i to order it on her Amazon Prime. It took 2 days for the rosin wire to arrive. When it arrived, it also came with a battery pack thing in a separate package. So I asked Papa: Can we solder the remote control car? But Papa was in the middle of cooking so he said no. So later when he was not busy, I asked can we solder the remote control car? Papa still said no, but he said I CAN! He got the soldering iron, the new rosin wire, and a cup with a wet towel in it to clean the soldering iron. I got the battery for the soldering iron. After some time the soldering was done! Now me and Papa just put in the wood pieces and attached the long wire. And then I tested it out and it worked!!! The remote control car actually worked!!! Then Mama tried and said it was so cool! The remote control car is so cool!
